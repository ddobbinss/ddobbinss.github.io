The Gemmatum Tang (Zebrasoma gemmatum) is a rare and striking surgeonfish, easily recognized by its jet-black body speckled with bright white or yellow spots and a vivid yellow tail. Native to the western Indian Ocean, it requires a spacious aquarium of at least 180 gallons with ample swimming space and rockwork for grazing. Primarily herbivorous, it thrives on a diet of marine algae, nori, and quality herbivore foods, and is considered reef-safe, helping control nuisance algae without disturbing corals. Best suited to experienced aquarists, the Gemmatum Tang is a true centerpiece species that rewards dedicated care with unmatched beauty.
